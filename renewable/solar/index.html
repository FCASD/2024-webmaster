Solar Panels

Solar Panels produce energy by converting sunlight (solar energy) into electricity through photovoltaic cells (PV cells), and the energy is then stored into a battery.

Benefits

Using solar panels can improve air quality, reduce the amount of water consumed from other forms of energy production, and other benefits.
