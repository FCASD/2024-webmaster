Wind Energy

Small wind energy systems called grid-connected systems can power your homes.

expand_more

Benefits

Grid connected wind turbine can reduce utility costs for lighting and appliances. Practicality can chance depending on your situation.






expand_more

Practicality

Off-grid hybrid electric wind system would be practical if:

  • the average annual wind speed in the area if at least 9 mph
  • Grid connection is not available
  • Like to generate independent electricity for utilities
  • Like to generate clean electricity
