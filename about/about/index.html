About Us

Sustainify is a company founded to revolutionize the homeowner's access to clean energy solutions.

Information about how to give back to our planet through green energy is often sparse and scattered. Even those who want to do good for the Earth are limited by this lack of accessible informatiom. At Sustainify, it is our goal to provide this information to all homeowners wishing to use clean energy solutions within their home.
