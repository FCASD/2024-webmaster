Why EVs?

Electric vehicles (EVs) offer many benefits to both you and others.

Savings on Money and Time

  • You can save 50% of your fuel expenses by driving an EV rather than a gasoline-powered vehicle.
  • Charging your car overnight at home will allow you to get through each day without ever needing to visit a gas station.
  • Plus, ensure that you charge your EV at home as much as possible, because this is less expensive than public charging, not to mention more convenient.

A Healthy You and a Clean Earth

  • EVs do not release any tailpipe emissions, preventing toxic chemicals from entering the air that goes into your lungs.
  • They also do not release greenhouse gasses, which are a major contributor to climate change.
  • EVs are much quieter than gas-powered vehicles, which helps reduce noise pollution.

Fuel Economy

  • EVs are almost always more efficient than gas-powered cars, traveling more distance with the same amount of energy.

Energy Security
