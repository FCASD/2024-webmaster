Clean Energy Practices

When it comes to green energy, it is important to note that there are methods to reduce present energy consumption levels so as to reduce demand for nonrenewable sources of energy production.

    Replacing Light Bulbs
    • Switch to energy-efficient light bulbs (using LEDs as opposed to incandescent lighting)
    • ENERGYSTAR® certification (90% less energy consumption than non-ENERGYSTAR® counterparts)

    Smart Power Strips
    • Reduce “phantom energy” (electricity given to electronics when powered off)

    Reducing Dependence on Traditional Water Heating
    • Use less warm water
    • Turn down your thermostat on water heater

    Weatherizing Home/Adding Insulation
    • Practice of sealing air leaks (usually around vents, windows, and doors)
    • Reduces amount of heating required
